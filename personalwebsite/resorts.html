Resorts

Brighton Resort

OVERVIEW: Known for its laid-back atmosphere and family-friendly vibe, Brighton Resort is a classic Utah favorite. With excellent snow coverage and a wide variety of runs, Brighton offers trails for all skill levels, making it a popular choice for both locals and visitors.

HIGHLIGHTS: This resort is one of the few where all terrain can be accessed by high-speed lifts, which means more skiing and less waiting. It also offers night skiing, giving skiers and snowboarders the chance to hit the slopes after dark.

BEST FOR: Families, beginners, and intermediate skiers looking for a relaxed, scenic skiing experience.

Snowbird Resort

OVERVIEW: Snowbird is a world-renowned resort famous for its steep terrain, deep powder, and stunning mountain scenery. Located in Little Cottonwood Canyon, it offers challenging trails and an impressive vertical drop, making it a top destination for experienced skiers.

Grand Targhee

OVERVIEW: Located in Wyoming, just outside of Jackson Hole, Grand Targhee is known for its relaxed vibe and impressive snowfall, often receiving over 500 inches per season. This hidden gem offers incredible powder, scenic views, and short lift lines.

HIGHLIGHTS: The resort is famous for its backcountry feel, with plenty of ungroomed runs and open bowls for powder lovers. It also provides stunning views of the Grand Tetons, and its relatively uncrowded slopes make it a great choice for skiers looking to avoid big crowds.

BEST FOR: Powder enthusiasts and intermediate to advanced skiers looking for an authentic, low-key experience.
